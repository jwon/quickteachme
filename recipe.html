Recipe for life

Ingredients

  • Willpower
  • socks
  • a friend

Directions

  1. Put on your socks on your feet. You don't want to have cold feet before you even start.
  2. Use your willpower to keep you motivated through life.
  3. If you start to get discouraged, ask your friend to help you. Life is always easier with another person!
  4. Realize that you're following a recipe for life and that no such thing exists.
